# cake-website-html
The major focus is to attract customers by very dynamic website that shows all the clients products and takes up order and purchase options. The website  consists of pages like home,gallery,blogs,webstore and contact us. The blogs put emphasis on the various feedbacks received by the client.  The web store is nothing a cart system where customer can choose their product and purchase it. The main focus is on home delivery. Hence when customer places an order the mail is received to Ms. Hiral stating the order delivery status. The customer too gets a repose of order being placed.
